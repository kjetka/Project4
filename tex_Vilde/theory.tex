= 4 \cosh\left( \beta 8 J \right) + 12

 The energy:
\[
\left< E \right> = k_B T^2 \left(\frac{\partial Z}{\partial T}
\right)_{V,N}

\[
= k_B T^2 \frac{\partial}{\partial T} \left[\ln \left(4\cosh \left(\frac{8J}{k_BT}\right) +12\right) \right]\]
